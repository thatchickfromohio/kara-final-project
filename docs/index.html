ABOUT THE BAND

Queen are a British rock band that formed in 1970 in London. Consisting of four members, the group went on to become a groundbreaking quad stylistically both in their sound and stage presence. Most recognized from their catalog are the smash hits “We Will Rock You”, “We are the Champions”, “Another One Bites the Dust” and “Bohemian Rhapsody”
